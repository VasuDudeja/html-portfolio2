My Top 3 Movies of all time

Death Note

One of the most intelligently written shows out there. I love the intellect of Light Yagami and L

Prison Break

An Engineer installs himself into the prison he has designed to save the life of his falsely convicted brother.

KGF Chapter 1

The hero is brought up in poverty. When he is very young itself he goes to Mumbai to become a mafia don. His mother's wish was to see her son rich and powerful.
